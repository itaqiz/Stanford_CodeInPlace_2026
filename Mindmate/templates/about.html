About This Project

What is MindMate?

MindMate is a minimal, thoughtfully designed emotional wellness check-in tool. It was built to provide a calm, judgment-free space for daily self-reflection — especially for students, developers, and knowledge workers who rarely pause to check in with themselves.

MindMate is not medical advice. It's a digital wellness companion — like a thoughtful friend who listens, suggests a breathing exercise, or shares a quote when you need it most.
